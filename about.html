About Me

Hi, my name is Joanne and I am an artist living in Derry, N.Ireland. I have always loved art and have been drawing and painting for as long as I can remember.

I am lucky enough to work as a community occupational therapist in Donegal and as part of my work I get to drive around the most beautiful areas in Ireland. I get much of my inspiration from this landscape. I have included some photos of the stunning areas that I derive inspiration from also.

The Peace Bridge, Derry

Inch Castle, Donegal

Grianán Fort, Inishowen

I love to paint in all mediums, especially oils and pastels. Lately I have been doing a lot of wildlife drawings and paintings and I have been exploring the medium of oil based coloured pencils and find them great to use as you can get some great, finer details in your work.

I do most of my painting and drawing in a small studio that I've set up in my home. You can see it in the picture here.
